european southern observatory photo gallery: http://www.eso.org/public/images/ (http://www.eso.org/ public/images/). magnificent color images from eso’s largest telescopes. see the topical menu at the top. hubble space telescope images: https://esahubble.org/images/ (https://esahubble.org/images/) click on the menu of categories or search for any object the telescope may have photographed. james webb space telescope images: https://esawebb.org/images/ (https://esawebb.org/images/) growing library of infrared images from jwst; use the menu of categories or search for an object you may have seen on the news. national optical astronomy observatories image gallery: http://www.noao.edu/image_gallery/ (http://www.noao.edu/image_gallery/). growing archive of images from the many telescopes that are at the united states’ national observatories. planetary photojournal: http://photojournal.jpl.nasa.gov/index.html (http://photojournal.jpl.nasa.gov/ index.html). features thousands of images from nasa’s extensive set of planetary exploration missions with a good search menu. does not include most of the missions from other countries.
