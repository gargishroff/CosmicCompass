(don’t get excited; these diamonds are only a billionth of a meter in size and would hardly make an impressive engagement ring!) the most widely accepted model pictures the grains with rocky cores that are either like soot (rich in carbon) or like sand (rich in silicates). in the dark clouds where molecules can form, these cores are covered by icy mantles (figure 20.16).
