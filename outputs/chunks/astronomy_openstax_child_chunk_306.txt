(a) the shadow cast by a spherical body (the moon, for example) is shown. notice the dark umbra and the lighter penumbra. four points in the shadow are labeled with numbers. in (b) you see what the sun and moon would look like in the sky at the four labeled points. at position 1, you see a total eclipse. at positions 2 and 3, the eclipse is partial.
