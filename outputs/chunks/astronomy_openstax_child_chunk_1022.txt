(a) this image shows both smooth and cratered terrain on saturn’s moon, and also “tiger stripes” in the south polar region (lower part of image). these dark stripes (shown here in exaggerated color) have elevated temperatures and are the source of the many geysers discovered on enceladus. they are about 130 kilometers long and 40 kilometers apart. (b) here enceladus is shown to scale with great britain and the coast of western europe, to emphasize that it is a small moon, only about 500 kilometers in diameter.
