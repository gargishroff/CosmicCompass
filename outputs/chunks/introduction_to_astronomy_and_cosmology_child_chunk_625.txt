one possible way to show the presence of wimp interactions in the presence of those caused by local radioactivity is due to the fact that, in june, the motion of the earth around the sun (29.6 km s!1) is in the same direction as that of the sun in its orbit around the galactic centre (232 km s!1). hence, the earth would sweep up more wimps than in december when the motions are opposed. the difference is ∼7%, so one might expect to detect more wimps in june than in december. as the number of interactions from local radioactivity should remain constant, this gives a possible means of making detection. in the dark matter (dama) experiment at the gran sasso national laboratory, 1400 m underground in italy, observations have been made of scintillations within 100 kg of pure sodium 322 introduction to astronomy and cosmology iodide crystals. the results of seven annual cycles have given what is regarded as a possible detection but, again, there may be other explanations. 9.12.4 dark energy normal and dark matter can between them account for some 27% of the total mass/energy of the universe.
