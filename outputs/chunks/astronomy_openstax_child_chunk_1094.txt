(credit: modification of work by nasa) one reason scientists are interested in the composition and interior structure of neas is that humans will probably need to defend themselves against an asteroid impact someday. if we ever found one of these asteroids on a collision course with us, we would need to deflect it so it would miss earth. the most straightforward way to deflect it would be to crash a spacecraft into it, either slowing it or speeding it up, slightly changing its orbital period.
