(b) in 2019, the event horizon telescope, a collaborative venture among radio telescopes spanning our planet, was able to produce the first radio image of the shadow of the m87 black hole on the accretion disk surrounding it. this was taken with radio waves, so color is arbitrary. the shadow is about 2 ½ times as big as the event horizon of the supermassive black hole.
