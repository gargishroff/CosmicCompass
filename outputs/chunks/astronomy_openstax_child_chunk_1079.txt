(a) near-earth asteroid bennu, imaged by the osiris-rex spacecraft in 2020. bennu is a small (half-km diameter), rapidly spinning dark asteroid. its low density suggests it is a loosely-bound rubble pile. the surface is very rough, with protruding rocks and boulders. (b) preparing to collect a sample, the collection arm is shown just before touch- down on bennu. for scale, the diameter of the sampling device is 30 cm. the sample of about 100 grams of surface rocks and dust will be returned to earth in 2023.
