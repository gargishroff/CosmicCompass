(credit: modification of work by pieter van dokkum, marijn franx (university of groningen/leiden), esa and nasa) active galactic nuclei and galaxy evolution while galaxy mergers are huge, splashy events that completely reshape entire galaxies on scales of hundreds of thousands of light-years and can spark massive bursts of star formation, accreting black holes inside galaxies can also disturb and alter the evolution of their host galaxies.
