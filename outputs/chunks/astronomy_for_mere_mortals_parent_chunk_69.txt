the most likely scenario at this time is that it was part of a double object which came too close to neptune: one piece was expelled from the vicinity while the other piece, triton, was captured gravitationally by neptune. as shown by figure 237, triton’s surface is very different from other moons and planets. due to the low temperatures, nitrogen freezes. it is also possible that triton’s core has kept the mantle warm enough for triton to have a liquid water subsurface ocean, similar to europa.
