the discovery of the cosmic microwave background (cmb) convinced the overwhelming majority of astrophysicists that the big bang theory had won the debate over the steady state theory. over the next few decades additional observations of quasars showed that they were not evenly distributed throughout the cosmos, confirming that the past was fundamentally different from the present. the perfect cosmological principle would have to be abandoned. the universe was not eternal.
