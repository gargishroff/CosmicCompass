(it is even colder in craters near the poles that receive no sunlight at all.) the range in temperature on mercury is thus 600 k (or 600 °c), a greater difference than on any other planet. what a difference a day makes mercury rotates three times for each two orbits around the sun. it is the only planet that exhibits this relationship between its spin and its orbit, and there are some interesting consequences for any observers who might someday be stationed on the surface of mercury. here on earth, we take for granted that days are much shorter than years.
