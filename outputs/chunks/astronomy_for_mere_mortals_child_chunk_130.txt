there is an additional form of radiation that is emitted when an electron that is excited with a spin that matches the proton in the hydrogen atom returns to ground state. it is called 21- centimeter radiation. this is part of the radio spectrum. molecules when you have atoms combined to form molecules there are more emission lines than you would expect from the atoms individually. the spectrum for molecules is more complex than for atoms.
