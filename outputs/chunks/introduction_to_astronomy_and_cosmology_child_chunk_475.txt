(3) as the star drops onto the main sequence, it goes through a very violent stage when approximately half its mass is lost. it is called the tau tauri phase in the life of the star. (tau tauri is a triple star system in taurus where the primary star is dropping onto the main sequence.) (4) the star settles on the main sequence burning hydrogen into helium. as the amount of helium in the core increases, its temperature rises and the star becomes somewhat hotter and so moves upward and to the left of the h–r diagram. figure 7.6 the evolutionary track of a 1 solar mass star on the h–r diagram.
