there is an envelope of hydrogen, helium, and methane gas surrounding this. the radius of the core is approximately 10,000 km. the thickness of the mantle is approximately 10,000 km and the thickness of the outer layer is approximately 5,000 km. neptune emits more energy than it receives from the sun, similar to saturn and jupiter. the cause of this excess radiation from neptune is not known. neptune’s axial tilt neptune’s rotational axis is tilted 28 degrees. this is not much different from earth’s. some storms can be seen in the cloud- tops of neptune in large telescopes. neptune’s outer atmosphere is very similar to jupiter’s. it is mostly molecular hydrogen with some helium and methane. unlike jupiter, there is very little ammonia.
